About Genesis

Genesis is the flagship event of IIC, designed to foster innovation and entrepreneurship. It provides a platform for aspiring entrepreneurs to showcase their ideas, receive mentorship, and compete for exciting prizes.

Genesis 2025 promises to be bigger and better with more participants, enhanced networking opportunities, and increased prize money.
